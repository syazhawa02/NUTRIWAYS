What is BMI

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure used to determine whether a person has a healthy weight, is underweight, or overweight. Deviating from the ideal weight range may increase health risks.

BMI Limitations

BMI does not account for body composition and may not be suitable for pregnant women, children, certain ethnic groups, or individuals with a muscular build. Additionally, age can affect BMI readings.

How to Calculate BMI?

Choose your gender and preferred measurement units in the BMI calculator below. Adjust the sliders to input your height and weight, then click 'calculate' to determine your BMI. The results will provide insights into what your BMI indicates.
